    SpaceGuessr Guide

    Pick a mode, study the image, and choose the answer that best matches the place or object in space.

    • Quick: short 3-round run.
    • Classic: the standard 5-round game.
    • Timed: 5 rounds with 10 seconds per image.
    • Infinite: keep going until you run out of misses.
    • Daily: one shared image per day.
