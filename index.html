    How it works Game rules

    Guess the object. Build score. Learn the patterns.

    Each round shows a NASA image or clue. Pick the right object, keep your streak alive, and watch out for the special mode twists.

    • Correct: +50 points in normal modes.
    • Blind Reveal: starts at +80 and drops with each reveal.
    • Learn: gives extra explanations and skips leaderboard pressure.
    • Reverse Learn: shows the clue first and reveals the image after your guess.
    • Streak: every continued streak adds +10 more points than the last.
    • Wrong: -25 points and your streak goes back to zero.
